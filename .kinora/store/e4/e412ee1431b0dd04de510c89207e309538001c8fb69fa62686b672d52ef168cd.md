# Task: GC/prune per policy + pin support

**Goal.** Enforce the per-root retention policies during compaction (root-entry GC + hot-ledger prune) and add the `pin: true` escape hatch that survives GC regardless of age or depth.

**Realizes:**
- [[kinora-xi21:spec-4]] — Multiple named roots, each with its own GC policy; inbox is the default.

**Plan:** [[kinora-hxmw:plan]]

**Done when:** `Never` drops nothing; `MaxAge(d)` drops unpinned entries/events older than `now - d`; `KeepLastN(n)` keeps the N newest store versions per kino id; `pin: true` exempts an entry and its named version (non-exclusive with the N-window); and pruning runs even on no-op compacts.

## Outcome
Resolved. `RootPolicy::max_age_seconds()` (checked_mul; `y=365d`) drives three new stages threaded through `compact_root`: `propagate_pins` (copies pin+version from prior root for still-owned kinos; pin wins over head, ownership wins over pin on cross-root moves), `apply_root_entry_gc` (MaxAge drops old unpinned entries; KeepLastN/Never no-op on the single-entry-per-kino root view), and `prune_hot_events` (MaxAge + KeepLastN drop old/excess store events; assigns age out by MaxAge only; root-kind events never pruned). GC uses `params.ts` as `now`. Distinction documented: policy controls what the root names, not what the immutable store holds. Cross-root reference protection deferred to f0rg. 9 new tests; 296 lib + 77 CLI green. Commits `a2efd0d`, `38cfeb7`, `5b75cda`.
