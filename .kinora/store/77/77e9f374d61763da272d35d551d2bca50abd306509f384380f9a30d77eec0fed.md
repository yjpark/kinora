# Prune staged events after archive, rebuild via prior-root merge

Once per-commit archives capture provenance, staged events for `Never`-policy
roots can be dropped after a successful archive — but a stateless `build_root`
would then lose every archived-and-pruned entry, so this settles how rebuild
recovers them.

## The decision
- **Chosen: prune owned staged events after archive for `RootPolicy::Never`** (the `commits` root and any user-declared Never root). Rejected leaving `prune_staged_events` a no-op for Never — staged kept growing unboundedly, defeating the point of "commit".
- **Chosen: make `build_root` take `prior_root: Option<&RootKinograph>` and merge in entries** whose id is missing from the fresh staged-only build AND that have no live-assign reassigning the kino elsewhere. The merge is fed prior_root only under Never policy; MaxAge/KeepLastN keep store events in staging so their stateless rebuild stays authoritative. Rejected merging unconditionally — it would resurrect naturally-superseded entries under retention policies.
- **Chosen: the live-reassign guard** prevents resurrecting a kino that moved roots after its store events were pruned (the surviving signal is the new assign).
- **Chosen: on the `commits` root drop ALL owned staged events** (archive store events + archive-assigns), not just the assigns the bean literally named — dropping only assigns wouldn't solve "staged keeps accumulating" and risks an orphaned archive store default-routing to inbox.
- **Chosen: keep `propagate_pins` AND add the merge** — complementary: pins propagate into entries present in the fresh build; merge adds entries absent from it.
- **Not a dead end:** a prior-root entry whose content blob is missing is merged anyway — blob reachability is a separate fsck-like concern, not rebuild's job. Generalizing the drain to MaxAge is the follow-up kino://5b3415a320224957e71ea61f63683c56b498f64abb1929e9bf8c512206b898e8.

## What this built
- `build_root` prior-root merge + `merge_source` Never gating, `maybe_archive_owned_events` returning `(archive_id, archived_event_hashes)`, a `drop_staged_events` helper (NotFound-tolerant for crash replay), and Never-prune wiring for both non-commits and commits roots. Verified by 7 new tests (end-to-end drains for both branches, merge-with-no-activity, merge-respects-reassign, plus 3 `build_root` unit tests) and a regression suite (498 tests, clean review).
